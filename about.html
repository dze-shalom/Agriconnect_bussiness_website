Our Story

From academic research to agricultural revolution

AgriConnect was born from watching her(Shalom) mother's world crumble. Growing up in rural Cameroon, Shalom watched her mother farm with the wisdom of generations - reading cloud patterns, feeling wind changes, timing planting by ancestral calendars. But around 2018, when Shalom was studying telecommunications engineering at the University of Buea, something changed. Climate change had broken the ancient rhythm, and the generational knowledge that had sustained their community for centuries was no match for unprecedented weather patterns.

For over a decade, that image haunted her. When she arrived at AIMS Cameroon in 2024 to study Data Science, she finally had the tools to honor her mother's wisdom while giving her the technological support she needed. When Shalom shared this deeply personal mission with her classmates, it sparked something powerful. Leslie, with her agricultural background, immediately understood. Mohamed, through his internship at NMD Satellite Technology, saw how satellite connectivity could reach the most remote farms. Roland's research expertise revealed how they could combine traditional knowledge with modern data science.

The breakthrough came when Mohamed realized they could combine satellite connectivity with IoT sensors to reach even the most remote farms. Shalom's telecommunications expertise provided the foundation for LoRa networks, while Leslie ensured their solutions addressed real farmer needs. Today, with Roland's research leadership and the continued support of AIMS Cameroon, we're developing what will become Africa's first comprehensive satellite-powered agricultural platform - bridging the gap between ancestral wisdom and technological innovation.
