Our Story

From Academic Research to Agricultural Innovation

AgriConnect began as a research project at AIMS Cameroon, where our team recognized the urgent need for technology solutions in African agriculture. While studying telecommunications, data science, AI, and climate science, we saw firsthand how farmers struggled with unpredictable weather, inefficient water usage, and limited access to agricultural information.

The breakthrough came when Mohamed, during his internship at NMD Satellite Technology, realized we could combine satellite connectivity with IoT sensors to reach even the most remote farms. Shalom's telecommunications expertise provided the foundation for our LoRa networks, while Leslie's agricultural background ensured our solutions addressed real farmer needs.

Today, with Roland's research leadership and the continued support of AIMS Cameroon, we're developing what will become Africa's first comprehensive satellite-powered agricultural platform. Our academic foundation gives us the rigor and innovation needed to solve complex agricultural challenges with cutting-edge technology.
