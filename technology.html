Complete Agricultural Intelligence System

Ground Layer: Smart Sensors

LoRa-powered sensor networks monitoring soil conditions, weather patterns, and crop health with real-time data collection across your entire farm.

Satellite Layer: NMD Connectivity

NMD satellite technology ensures reliable data transmission even in remote areas, providing weather intelligence and enabling precision agriculture anywhere in Cameroon.

AI Layer: Smart Decision Making
