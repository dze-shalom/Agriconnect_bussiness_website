Pilot Program Requirements

We're looking for progressive farmers committed to agricultural innovation

Farm Requirements

  • Location: South West Region, Cameroon
  • Size: Minimum 0.5 hectares under cultivation
  • Crops: Vegetables, fruits, or cash crops (diversified preferred)
  • Water Access: Reliable water source for irrigation
  • Goals: Interest in increasing productivity and efficiency

Farmer Requirements

  • Technology: Smartphone with internet access
  • Education: Basic literacy in French or English
  • Commitment: 6-month program participation
  • Feedback: Weekly progress reports and testimonials
  • Attitude: Openness to new farming methods
